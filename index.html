About me

I received my B.S degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018 and my Ph.D degree from University of Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2023.
I am currently an assistant professor at School of Statistics and Data Science, Nankai University.
My research interests are sampling & stochastic optimizating, graph neural networks and their applications, and deep learning theory.
